About Me

Nicholas Zaidan

Graduate from the University of Sydney with a Bachelors of Computer Science and Information Technology. Finished with a double major in Computer Science and Information Systems.

Moved to Belgium at the age of 2 and attended the British School of Brussels before moving to Hong Kong 6 years later. Here I completed my high school education at the Australian International School Of Hong Kong, finishing my Higher School Certificate.

I then returned home to Australia after 16 years overseas to complete my education at the University of Sydney with a Bachelors.
